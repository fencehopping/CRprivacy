Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Constant Reader (“the App,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the App.

Simple summary: We designed this app to collect as little information as possible.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not store your personal data on our servers unless explicitly stated in the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information through the normal use of the App.

Any information you provide in the App is processed only as needed to deliver the App’s functionality. Unless otherwise clearly disclosed inside the App, that information is not retained after processing.

2. No Account Required

You can use the App without creating an account. We do not require registration in order to access the core functionality of the App.

3. Data Storage

We do not maintain user profiles or permanently store uploaded content, personal information, or app activity on our servers unless specifically described in the App experience.

If the App processes data you provide, that processing is intended to be temporary and limited to what is necessary to provide the requested result.

4. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use third-party advertising trackers to follow you across apps or websites.

We do not sell your personal information.

If this app later adds analytics, crash reporting, or similar tools, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those tools are used.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on Apple-provided infrastructure or other service providers strictly as needed to operate the App. If a third-party service is used in a way that involves data collection, that use will be disclosed in an updated version of this Privacy Policy and reflected in App Store privacy disclosures.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will address the issue promptly.

7. Data Security

Because we aim not to retain personal data, we reduce many of the privacy risks associated with long-term storage. Even so, we take reasonable steps to protect the App and any limited data processing involved in delivering its functionality.

8. Your Choices

Because we generally do not store personal data, there is typically no account data for you to access, modify, or delete.

If you contact us directly and share information voluntarily, you may request that we delete that correspondence, subject to any legal obligations.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.
